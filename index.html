What we do

We turn youth commitment into structured impact.

From Solidarity Action Day to community projects and mental health initiatives, ActivEU builds programmes that combine youth leadership, institutional reliability and measurable outcomes.

SAD
Solidarity Action Day (SAD)
One day of lessons becomes one day of structured work in host organisations. The value generated supports social causes, while students gain experience, visibility and a concrete sense of contribution. In 2026, SAD is a controlled pilot focused on testing workflows, evaluation tools and certification mechanisms before scaling.
Digital
ActivEU Connect
The digital backbone for fair, secure and meaningful matches between students, host organisations and causes. In 2026, it runs as an internal infrastructure to stress-test verification, data protection, matching criteria and algorithmic fairness before public deployment.
Local impact
Community projects with municipalities
Long-term partnerships that activate public space, connect generations and document local realities. Projects like “Raízes e Risos” and “Tradições em Páginas” combine youth volunteering with structured methodologies and outputs that municipalities can reuse.
Well-being
ActivEU: Respira
An initiative focused on mental health and well-being for young people aged 13–25. It combines individual psychological support, group sessions and complementary activities, always prioritising ethics, safety and accessibility.
